How to Activate Your TV Service

  1. Connect your TV to the internet
  2. Turn on your TV device
  3. Go to Settings > Account > Activation
  4. Note down the activation code shown on your screen
  5. Enter the activation code and your account details
  6. Click Activate and within 30 seconds your TV will refresh
